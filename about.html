Our Philosophy

Brewing with
Intention.

We believe a coffee shop should be an extension of your living room—a place where ideas brew as slowly and richly as our signature roasts.

Est. 2019

The Beginning

Founded in a small garage with a 5kg roaster and a dream. We rejected the fast-paced, grab-and-go culture in favor of something more deliberate.

It started with a simple question: What if we treated coffee like wine? Considering terroir, process, and the human hands behind every bean.

2020 - Present

Expansion & Community

Despite the global pause, we expanded to our flagship roastery. It became a hub not just for coffee, but for connection.

We launched the "Bloom Arts" initiative, transforming our walls into a gallery for local photographers and our evenings into poetry slams.
